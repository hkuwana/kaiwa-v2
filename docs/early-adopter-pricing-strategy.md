# Kaiwa Early Adopter & Product Advisor Program

**Strategic Pricing & Structure for First 20 Users**

---

## The Problem You're Solving

As an early-stage founder, you need:
1. **Engaged users** who actually use the product (not just sign up)
2. **Honest feedback** on what works and what doesn't
3. **Testimonials & case studies** for marketing
4. **Revenue validation** (even small amounts prove willingness to pay)
5. **Product direction** (what features to build next)

You DON'T need:
- Passive users who sign up and ghost
- Maximum revenue right now (you need learning more)
- People who want "everything for free forever"

---

## Recommended Tier Structure

### Tier 1: "Founding Member" (10 spots) - $14/month

**What they get**:
- Premium features (normally $29/month)
- **Lifetime price lock** at $14/month (NEVER goes up, even as you add features)
- Weekly Practice Labs (group sessions)
- 1 personal 1-on-1 session per month (30 min)
- Direct access to you (Slack/Discord/WhatsApp group)
- "Founding Member" badge in app
- First access to all new features
- Name in "Hall of Fame" on website (if they want)

**What you get from them**:
- **Required**: Use Kaiwa 2x per week minimum (trackable)
- **Required**: Monthly 15-min feedback call with you
- **Required**: Document 1 real conversation per month (voice memo, transcript, or journal)
- **Requested**: Testimonial when they have a win
- **Requested**: Refer 1 friend (not required, but encouraged)

**Why $14 not free**:
- Free = no commitment, people ghost
- $14 = "I'm serious about this" signal
- Still feels like an amazing deal (50% off Premium)
- You validate people WILL pay for this

**Why 10 spots**:
- Small enough to give everyone personal attention
- Large enough to get diverse feedback
- Creates urgency ("Only 10 founding spots!")

---

### Tier 2: "Product Advisor" (10 spots) - $19/month

**What they get**:
- Premium features (normally $29/month)
- **Lifetime price lock** at $19/month
- Weekly Practice Labs (group sessions)
- Quarterly 1-on-1 check-in (15 min)
- Product Advisor Discord/Slack channel
- Early access to new features (1 week before general release)

**What you get from them**:
- **Required**: Use Kaiwa 1x per week minimum
- **Required**: Quarterly feedback survey (detailed)
- **Requested**: Vote on feature priorities
- **Requested**: Beta test new features
- **Requested**: Testimonial when appropriate

**Why $19 not $14**:
- Less intensive commitment on their side
- Still 33% off Premium
- Validates your standard "Plus" pricing ($19 is your future baseline)

**Why 10 spots**:
- Gives you 20 total engaged users (statistically meaningful)
- Different commitment level attracts different personas
- You can A/B test messaging between groups

---

### Tier 3: "Plus" (Unlimited after first 20) - $19/month

**What they get**:
- Plus features (standard tier)
- Weekly Practice Labs
- Community access

**What you get**:
- Standard monthly subscription
- No special commitment required

**When to open this**:
- After your first 20 (Founding + Advisor) spots are filled
- OR after Week 4 of your 30-day plan
- This becomes your standard offering

---

### Tier 4: "Premium" (Launch later) - $29/month

**What they get**:
- All Plus features
- Unlimited scenarios (Plus might have limits)
- Advanced features (custom scenarios, speech analysis, etc.)
- Priority support

**When to launch**:
- Month 2-3, after you've built premium features
- Or when you have clear differentiation from Plus

## Smart Moves

### 1. Grandfather Clause (Critical)

**Promise**: "Your price is locked FOREVER. Even when we add features, even when we raise prices, even when we're huge - you pay $14/month for life."

**Why this works**:
- Creates insane loyalty
- They become your biggest advocates
- They feel like VIPs
- Word-of-mouth marketing is priceless

**Example**: Buffer did this with their first 1000 customers. Those customers are STILL evangelists 10+ years later.

---

**For Founding Members**:

- You're helping them succeed (not just taking money)

## FAQ for Yourself

### "What if I can't deliver on all the features I promise?"

**Answer**: You're not promising specific features. You're promising:
- Access to what exists now
- Early access to what you build
- A voice in what gets built

As long as you're transparent ("We're building X next based on your feedback"), you're good.

---

### "What if people abuse the lifetime lock?"

**Answer**:
- It's only 20 people max ($300-400/month "loss" vs $29/mo pricing)
- The feedback they give you is worth WAY more than $15/month
- If they're not engaged (don't use it), they naturally churn anyway
- The marketing value of "Original Founding Members" is priceless

---

### "Should I offer refunds?"

**Answer**:
- Yes, make it easy: "First month doesn't work out? Full refund, no questions asked."
- After Month 1: Standard monthly billing (they can cancel anytime)
- This removes risk for them and shows confidence in your product

---

### "What if I only get 5 founding members, not 10?"

**Answer**:
- 5 engaged users > 50 passive users
- Keep the spots open but raise urgency: "Only 5 spots left!"
- Adjust pricing later if needed (you can always add Product Advisor tier)

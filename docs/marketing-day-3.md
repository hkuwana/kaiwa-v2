## Pro Tips for Video Content

1. **Don't aim for perfection**: Authentic > polished
2. **Hook in 2 seconds**: Start with the problem or the promise
3. **Use text overlays**: Many people watch without sound
4. **End with a question**: Drives comments (which boosts algorithm)
5. **Post at peak times**: 6-9am, 12-2pm, 7-10pm (your timezone)
